About Me

Passionate developer with a love for creating innovative solutions

Building the Future, One Line at a Time

I believe the best digital products are created by fusing the newest technology with human-centered design. Hi, my name is Andersen Johannes Putra Susanto and I am currently in my fifth semester of Computer Science at BINUS University, with a GPA of 3.18. This conviction fuels my passion for Front-End Development, aimed at creating web experiences that are aesthetically pleasing, user-friendly, and accessible.

Frontend development constitutes the foundation of my technical repertoire. I employ HTML, CSS, React.js, and Three.js to create dynamic and interactive user interfaces. I am proficient in backend development utilizing Node.js and MySQL, which enhances my comprehension of the entire application. I possess proficiency in Figma and AxureRP for designing user interfaces and experiences, ensuring my work is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

In my spare time from coding, I enhance my communication and collaboration abilities as an activist in the Public Relations Division for Volleyball Binus. This experience has imparted significant insights regarding collaboration and decision-making. I am eager to apply these skills in a professional setting.
